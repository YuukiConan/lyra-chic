About Us

Lyra was built when...

Keyza Richi and Yuuki Conan at the same high school were thinking of ways to make Richi popular thanks to her interior design and fashion, which are aesthetic, precise, yet practical for everyone. Their idea to build Lyra required a great deal of effort, as Yuuki was the sole initial developer of this website.

After a few months, Keisya Ayu joined the team to make the website accessible to international visitors, with flexible language localization features. Today, Lyra is the leading website for finding and recommending interior design, fashion, and graphic design ideas in Indonesia.
